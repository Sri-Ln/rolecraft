## AI collaborator

## How we work

The design is brainstormed in dialogue. Specs and implementation plans live under `docs/superpowers/` and are committed before any code. Claude drafts; the human reviews and approves. No code is committed unless the human signs off on the change.

If you fork rolecraft and contribute, add yourself here.
